Our Vision

We are a trading specialist that trade regional and narrow body aircraft. Over time, Formidion will also develop its own leasing platform and will actively identify appropriate aircraft to transition onto its own platform

Founding management have developed strong relationships over a collective 55-year period having worked with many of the worlds largest aircraft leasing, airline, OEM and MRO companies. This will allow Formidion to scale rapidly and capitalise on management’s access to highly profitable aircaft.

We benefit from being truly at the epicentre of the aviation ecosystem, in Dublin, Ireland. Dublin is home to the world’s top aircraft lessors, airlines and aviation advisors

The fluidity that we can offer to assess opportunities and transact in a short space of time make us an attractive transaction partner to many lessors, airlines and trading partners
