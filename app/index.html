Meet the Team

Michael Bannon

PGA Professional Michael Bannon is one of Europe’s top teaching professionals and is the current coach of 4 time major winner and World Number 1, Rory McIlroy whom he has coached from a young age.
Golf is a passion for Michael and he takes as much satisfaction from teaching young players and beginners as he does teaching top players, rooting his beliefs in sound basics and simplicity of the swing.

Geoff Loughrey

Geoff trained with Michael to become a fully qualified PGA Pro. Having trained with Michael, both were presented with awards for Geoff’s outstanding achievement in examinations at the PGA HQ in The Belfry. Geoff and Michael work closely with many young international players to develop and achieve their potential.
