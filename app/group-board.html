John Caslin - Independant Non Executive

John is the chairman of the Banking and Aviation Finance Committee of the Society of Actuaries in Ireland.
John is a Fellow of the Society of Actuaries in Ireland and a former chairman of the Society’s Investment & Finance Committee
He has served as a director of iShares plc, an index and ETF fund manager; a director of Alder Capital, a systematic, target-risk currency and equity fund manager; and as a member of the investment committee of the board of Eagle Star Life (now Zurich).
John’s name has been entered in the Prizes’ Book of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries for two papers including one entitled Hedge Funds. He holds a First Class Honours Degree and Gold Medal in Engineering from Trinity College and a Diploma in Aviation Leasing and Finance from the Law Society of Ireland.

He is currently the Head of Investment Innovation at Aviva Life and Pensions Ireland.

Al Young - Technical & Asset Management

With 16+ years in Aircraft Leasing Al has held a number of senior roles in Aircraft Leasing and Aircraft Asset Management while he was Vice President Global Operations and Chief Commercial Officer at Avmax Aviation Services located at Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

Al has spent over 25 years of diverse experience in aerospace operations and commenced his career as an aircraft technician in the Royal Canadian Air Force. He went onto to obtain his Aircraft Maintenance Engineer licence and Commercial pilots licence.

Al was instrumental in setting up airline and maintenance operations in Africa owned by Avmax and has worked closely with many regional airlines customers from around the world.
